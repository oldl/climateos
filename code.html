THE VISIONARY

Built by a Brussels maker.

Olivier "Olo" is a developer and urbanist dedicated to radical transparency in climate data. After years of working with complex governmental APIs, he realized the missing link wasn't the data itself, but its accessibility to the average citizen.

"I wanted to transform climate data into tools people can actually use. In the face of a warming planet, information isn't just power—it's safety."
Olivier "Olo", Founder ClimateOS
